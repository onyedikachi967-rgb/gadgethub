Refund & Return Policy

Last Updated: February 2024

At GadgetHub, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. This policy outlines our return and refund procedures.

1. Return Eligibility

1.1 Timeframe

  • You have 7 days from the date of delivery to initiate a return
  • Returns requested after 7 days will not be accepted
  • The return window begins when you receive the product

1.2 Product Condition

To be eligible for a return, items must be:

  • Unused and in original condition: Products must not show signs of use
  • Original packaging: All boxes, manuals, accessories, and packaging materials must be included
  • With all accessories: Chargers, cables, earphones, etc. must be returned
  • With proof of purchase: Order confirmation or receipt required
  • Unaltered: No modifications or repairs attempted

1.3 Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Opened software, games, or downloadable content
  • Personal care items (headphones, earbuds used/opened)
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable"
  • Customized or personalized products
  • Gift cards or digital products
  • Items damaged due to misuse or neglect

2. Valid Reasons for Returns

We accept returns for the following reasons:

  • Defective Products: Manufacturing defects or malfunctions
  • Wrong Item: We shipped the incorrect product
  • Damaged in Transit: Product arrived damaged
  • Missing Items: Accessories or parts missing from the package
  • Not as Described: Product significantly differs from description
  • Changed Mind: You no longer want the item (subject to conditions)

3. Return Process

3.1 How to Initiate a Return

  1. Contact Us:
    • Email: returns@gadgethub.online
    • Phone: [Your phone number]
    • Provide your order number and reason for return
  2. Return Authorization:
    • We will review your request within 24-48 hours
    • If approved, you'll receive a Return Authorization Number (RAN)
    • Returns without RAN may not be accepted
  3. Package the Item:
    • Pack the product securely in its original packaging
    • Include all accessories, manuals, and original receipts
    • Write the RAN clearly on the package
  4. Ship the Return:
    • Ship to the address we provide
    • Use a trackable shipping method
    • Keep your tracking number for reference

3.2 Return Shipping Costs

  • Defective/Wrong Item: We cover return shipping costs
  • Change of Mind: Customer is responsible for return shipping
  • Damaged in Transit: We cover all costs

4. Refund Process

4.1 Inspection

  • Once we receive your return, we will inspect it within 3-5 business days
  • We will verify the product condition and eligibility
  • You will receive an email notification of approval or rejection

4.2 Refund Method

Approved refunds are processed as follows:

  • Card Payments: Refunded to original payment card (5-10 business days)
  • Bank Transfer: Refunded to your bank account (3-7 business days)
  • Cryptocurrency: Refunded to your crypto wallet (1-3 business days)

4.3 Refund Amount

  • Full Refund: For defective, wrong, or damaged items
  • Partial Refund: If item shows signs of use or missing accessories
  • Restocking Fee: 10% restocking fee may apply for change-of-mind returns
  • Shipping Costs: Original shipping fees are non-refundable (except for our errors)

5. Exchanges

  • We do not offer direct exchanges
  • To exchange an item, return it for a refund and place a new order
  • This ensures you get the correct item quickly

6. Damaged or Defective Items

6.1 Upon Delivery

If you receive a damaged or defective item:

  • Inspect the package immediately upon delivery
  • Take photos of the damage (packaging and product)
  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • We will arrange for a replacement or refund

6.2 After Use

If a defect appears after initial use:

  • Contact us immediately with details of the defect
  • Provide photos or videos if possible
  • We will determine if it's covered under warranty or our return policy

7. Warranty Claims

  • Many products come with manufacturer warranties
  • For warranty claims, contact the manufacturer directly
  • We will assist with warranty documentation and claims
  • Keep your receipt and warranty card for warranty service
